How OracleX Works

Fully autonomous from price signal to settlement — no human in the loop

◈
Step 1

Signal Detected

AI Signal Agent watches OKX price feeds and detects volatility windows worth betting on.

⬡
Step 2

Market Deployed

Creator Agent deploys a PredictionMarket contract on X Layer with initial OKB liquidity.

◎
Step 3

Place Your Bet

Connect your wallet and bet OKB on YES or NO. Odds update in real-time as the pool shifts.

✓
Step 4

Auto Settlement

At deadline, Resolver Agent fetches OKX price and settles on-chain. Winners claim proportional pool rewards.

ERC-8183 · Job Commit / Complete Hash

Every market deployment records a keccak256 hash of the job parameters on-chain. Every resolution records a completion hash, enabling cryptographic audit of every agent action.

ERC-8004 · Agent Reputation Score

Creator and Resolver Agent reputation scores increment with each successful operation. Scores are publicly verifiable on X Layer explorer. Slashing is triggered on failures.
